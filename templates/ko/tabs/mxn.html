TO FUND/REDEEM MEXICAN PESO:

Register or login at Bitso.com to initiate deposits and withdrawals.

  • A valid, non-US, state-issued ID (passport, driver's license, etc).
  • Proof of address (utility bill or other type of bill that has your address on it). Verification is required for peso deposits or withdrawals.
  • To fund your Ripple account, see the below options. Once a balance is deposited with Bitso, you can send it to your Ripple account using the Bitso Dashboard.
  • To withdraw from your Ripple account, click on the 'Redeem from Ripple' button on the Bitso Dashboard.
DEPOSIT OPTIONS

Bitso accepts deposits by the following methods:

  • Bank Wire
  • SPEI (Mexican instant inter-bank transfer)
  • Bitcoin
  • MXN Cash (at locations across Mexico)
  • Pademobile wallet
WITHDRAWAL OPTIONS

Bitso offers withdrawals via the following methods:

  • Bank Wire
  • SPEI (Mexican instant inter-bank transfer)
  • Bitcoin
